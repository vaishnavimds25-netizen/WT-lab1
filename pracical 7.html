Tribute to Sushant Singh Rajput

Sushant Singh Rajput was a celebrated Indian actor known for his versatile roles in films like MS Dhoni: The Untold Story, who significantly impacted India through his passion for education, science, and philanthropy. He funded NASA training for underprivileged children, supported flood relief, promoted STEM education, and partnered with Niti Aayog for women's entrepreneurship.Rajput was reportedly an avid reader who was deeply interested in astrophysics and won the National Olympiad in Physics. He secured admission in the Delhi College of Engineering(later renamed Delhi Technological University) to pursue a Bachelor of Engineering degree in mechanical engineering.

ACHIEVEMENTS:

  • A former National Olympiad Winner in Physics with a 7th rank in AIEEE.
  • Best Debut Nominee
  • Studied Mechanical Engineering at the esteemed Delhi College of Engineering (DCE/DTU) before pursuing acting.
